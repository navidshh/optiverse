FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before enrolling.

Where are the courses hosted, and what do I get with each?

Every course is published on Udemy. When you enrol, you get lifetime access, downloadable resources, all future updates, and a certificate of completion. Udemy also offers a 30-day money-back guarantee, so you can try any course risk-free.

I’m new to this. Are the courses beginner-friendly?

Yes. Most courses start with foundational modules and gradually build toward hands-on projects. Prerequisites are clearly listed on each course page — basic Python and comfort with the command line are helpful for AI/ML courses, and no prior simulation experience is required for the energy modules.

Which course should I start with?

It depends on your goal. For AI & ML, start with a foundations course and then branch into Generative AI / LLMs. For energy & simulation, EnergyPlus fundamentals is the natural entry point, then move on to HOMER or optimization. Not sure? Use the contact form and I’ll suggest a path based on your background.

Do you offer live workshops for teams and companies?

Yes. I run tailored workshops — virtual, on-site, or hybrid — for engineering, R&D, and data-science teams. Duration typically ranges from a half-day intro to a multi-day deep dive. Head to the Request a Workshop section to get in touch.

Do I get code, datasets, and project templates?

Yes. Every course ships with the full source code, sample datasets, and any project templates used in the lectures. You’re free to reuse them in your own work.

Can I ask questions while I’m going through a course?

Absolutely. Udemy has a built-in Q&A on every course — I read and respond there personally. For workshop enquiries or collaborations, use the contact form.
