Meet your instructor

Hi, I'm Navid — I teach the tools I use every day.

I'm an Applied Scientist with a Ph.D. in Engineering and a decade of experience at the intersection of AI, optimization, and energy systems. My work focuses on scalable ML and optimization tools that accelerate building energy modeling, urban infrastructure planning, and digital twins.

I currently work at Natural Resources Canada, where I contribute to national AI initiatives — surrogate modeling tools, LLM fine‑tuning for energy efficiency, and cloud‑deployed analytics platforms. My background spans hybrid modeling techniques that bridge physics‑based simulations with deep learning, deployed on AWS for real‑world impact.

I thrive in interdisciplinary environments where engineering, data science, and cloud technologies converge to solve complex sustainability challenges — and I built OptiVerse Academy to share those methods with you.
